About the ACM

The Association of Computing Machinery brings together Professionals, Educators, and Students alike under the goal of recognizing technical excellence and advancing technical achievement. This is done by maintaining strong leadership qualities and inspiring members through exemplary achievements met by others of the community.The ACM is designed to encourage life-long learning, strong career development, and professional networking. On a larger scale, the ACM is a worldwide organization, with large amounts of its membership stretching into Europe, India, and China, making it a representation of technological unity.

There are many ways the SeLU ACM allows its members to get involved. These include, and are not limited to, monthly scheduled meetings, talks from various guest speakers about many diverse topics, ACM cookouts, coding and development competions, and even "SLUGo!", our semesterly 12 hour gaming marathon.

Besides these events, there are many benefits to becoming a member of Southeastern's ACM Chapter.

These include:

  • Access to every ACM meeting
  • Access to free food and drinks provided at meetings
  • Ability to network with other members
  • Direct interaction with people working in the CSIT field
  • Exclusive first looks into local internships
  • Free subscription to the ACM newsletter
  • A really nice resume builder!

For additional information and/or questions on becoming a member, visit the "Contact Us" page for officer information. For more information regarding the Official ACM, visit their website at acm.org
